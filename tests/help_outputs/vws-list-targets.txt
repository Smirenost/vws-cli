Usage: vws list-targets [OPTIONS]

  List targets.

  See
  https://library.vuforia.com/articles/Solution/How-To-Use-the-Vuforia-Web-Services-API.htm#How-To-Get-a-Target-List-for-a-Cloud-Database.
